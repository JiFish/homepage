Astro Ball

A Lunar Pool/Ball Clone

Astro Ball is a game I wrote inspired by the 8-bit classic Lunar Pool. Sink balls on to progress through tables and score points. Either single player or competing with up to 6 players.

Features

  • 5 tables, plus an additional 20 to unlock
  • Authentic strange physics.
  • CRT-filters for a retro feel.
  • Secrets to discover.
  • Chiptune grooves from rolemusic.

Gameplay

Astro Ball attempts to replicate Lunar Pool's unique feel. The physics are closer to air hockey than a traditional ball game. Tables vary in shape and ball placement. Balls can be sunk in any order. The number on the ball represents it's values in points. Each player has 3 balls and can lose a ball put sinking the cue ball or failing to sink another ball 3 turns in a row.
